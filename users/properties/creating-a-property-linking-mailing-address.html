Creating a Property and Linking a Mailing Address

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Start on the dashboard.

2. Click "property search & groups"

3. Click "Add new property"

4. Enter in the county parcel ID.

5. Click "Create property and go to profile"

6. Here you will se the parcel ID, but no mailing address.

7. Scroll down to "Mailing Address Links".

8. Click "Link New Mailing Address"

9. Search by zip code first.

10.

11. Click "load streets"

12. If the street does not already exist, click "New street in 15147"

13. Enter the street name and click "Save Changes"

Tip: Tip! After adding the street name, you'll have to search it again.

14. Click this search field.

15. Type the street name.

16. Click "load mailing addresses"

17. Click "New mailing address on COGLAND TEST"

18. Type the building number.

19. Click "Save changes"

Tip: Tip! You'll have to search for the building number you just created.

20. Click "select for linking"

21. Click "Link Parcel ID :123-456-789-10"

22. Click "Choose an association type"

23. Click "property address"

24. Click "Save changes"

25. And now you will have a new property with its county parcel ID and mailing address linked.

Tip: Tip! You won't have to go through this process for each property in your muni. You would only have to do this occasionally - for new builds or if a property was missed in the scrape from the county site.
