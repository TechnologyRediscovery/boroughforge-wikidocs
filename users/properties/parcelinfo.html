Property info: 

The most direct way to designate properties as rentals, multi-units, etc. is through the Property Info box.

  • Navigate to your desired property page
  • On the left column, find Property Summary and click edit property info
  • This will bring up a dialog where you have can configure so much!
    1. You'll want to look at the drop down box on the left called Use Type where you can select the desired use type.
    2. On the right you can also label a property with special flags for Unfitness of occupancy, Vacancy, and Abandonment. 
      1. When you're flagging these three conditions, you enter start and stop dates so you can maintain a record of when the condition began and ended
  • When you save the changes, you'll see the new info on the property profile page
