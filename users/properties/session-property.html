Session Property

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. The property tab here shows the "Session Property". In some municipalities, this is the address of the borough building.

2. Clicking the address will bring you to the addresses' property page.

3. To change the session property, search for a different address. Navigate to the Table of Contents to review on searching for a property.

4. After searching for a new property, you can see that the information in the property box changed, but also the information in the

5. person box...

Tip: Tip! This may not always be the owner information but someone else associated with the property. By clicking on the property and scrolling to the bottom left, you can see all the people associated with the property and their roles.

6. and the CE (Code Enforcement) Case box also changed.

7. Click "view permit file"...

8. to load a permit file associated with the property.

9. Click "cases on sess prop"...

10. to see the "Open case list".
