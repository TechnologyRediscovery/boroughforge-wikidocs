Property Search

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Start on the dashboard.

2. Click "property search & groups".

3. Click this drop down menu to pick a search critera.

4. These are the options for property search.

5. The most frequently used will be building number alone, but here the building number and street name search is selected. This can be helpful if you need to refine a search.

6. Enter the building number and street name for the property you want to view.

7. Click "Search"

Tip: Tip! Different search functions will result in various amounts of search results. Its up to the user to find their preference for search criteria.

8. When the search results load on the right side of the screen, find the needed property, and click "view property".

9. And then you will be brought to the property landing page.

Property Search - Path Two

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Start on the dashboard and scroll down to below the event calendar.

2. Click this text field to enter the property number.

3. And this field to enter the street name. But, you can search one at a time if you prefer.

4. Click "Search"

5. To the left of the search boxes is where the search results will appear.

6. Click "view property"

7. to redirect to the property landing page.

8. To demonstrate what more search results will look like, I changed municipalities.

9. Click this text field to enter the building number.

10. Click "Search"

11. And this is what more results will look like.

12. You can reverse the order by clicking "reverse order".
