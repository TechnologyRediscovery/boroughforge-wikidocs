Add a Property Alert

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Start on a property landing page.

2. Scroll down to the events box and click "Add Event"

3. Click "Property alerts"

4. Select the alert you would like to add to the property.

5. Click here to add a description.

6. Click here...

7. to choose the date when you are setting the alert.

8. Click "Create new event"

9. Click the property address to refresh the page.

10. Now, the alert will appear on the property landing page.

Tip: Tip! Adding these alerts can be helpful in letting other officers know about potentially dangerous conditions.
