Connecting People to Properties

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Start on the property landing page.

2. Scroll down to the "Person Links" box and click "Manage Links".

3. Click "Create new person".

4. Enter the name of the person you're creating.

5. You can also enter their job title, select if they are a multi-person name entry, or a business name.

6. Click "Save changes".

7. Click "Connect person to current property 456 COGLAND TEST".

8. Click "Choose an association type".

9. Select the association type that best fits the role the person has with the property.

10. Click "Create link".

Tip: Tip! Refresh the page in your browser to see the person link at the top of the page or listed in the person links box (depending on the association type).

Tip: Tip! Make sure the mailing addresses are updated for any association type that would not get mail at the property (property managers, management companies, non-occupant owners, etc).
