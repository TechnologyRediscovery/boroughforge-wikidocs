Updating Person Property Links

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Start on the property landing page.

2. Scroll down to the "person links" box.

3. Click "View/Edit link" for the person whose role you're changing.

4. Click "Edit".

5. Change their role from "owner-occupant of"...

6. to "formerly owned".

7. Click "Save changes"

8. Close the link pop-up

9. Click "Manage Links".

10. Search for the new person link first.

Tip: Tip! Searching before adding a new person can help you find people flagged for PA Act 90 or simply prevent duplicate people in the system.

11. If the person you searched for has no other property links in the system...

12. then go ahead and "Create new person".

13. Enter their name and click "Save changes".

14. Click "Connect person to current property 456 COGLAND TEST".

15. Click "Choose an association type".

16. Select their role.

17. Click "Create link".

18. Click "Connect person to Mailing Address" if need be.

Tip: Tip! This process is common for deed transfers or change of occupancy permits (if required by your municipality). Its important to keep these updated as if you need to issue a citation, it needs to be in the owner's name for legal purposes.
