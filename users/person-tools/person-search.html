Person Search

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. The person search can be started from anywhere. Navigate up to the person tab on the top menu bar and click "person search/add person".

2. Selecting the drop down menu, you can see different criteria to search by, but "name" works most of the time.

3. Enter the name of the person you are searching for.

Tip: Tip! Person records are available across all municipalities that use CodeNForce.

4. Click "search".

5. Click "view" to see the person's profile.

6.

7. On the person's profile, you can see the person's connections to properties, their mailing address,...

8. as well as any contact information you may have for them.
