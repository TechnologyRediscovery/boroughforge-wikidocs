# Persons
"Persons" in CodeNForce are any and all people or busniesses assiocated with a property within a municipality using CodeNForce. Individuals like homeoweners, tenants, or property managers are entered into the system the same as businesses. Using the person search function will not only bring up an individual homeowner, but a busniess that might share a name. For example, if you serach for "Jones", you could get results back for both Frank Jones, a homeowner, and Jones Realty, a property manamgement group. These two person entries may have nothing to do with each other, but both could come up in a search. 

The person profiles are searchable across municipal lines. CodeNForce allows for municipal staff in differnt towns see if an actor has a case open or even citations filed against them in another municicpality. Data sharing helps towns leverage PA Act 90 - allowing municipalities to deny sales of property to those with negative histories. 

Person linking is key to the functionality of CodeNForce. Municipal staff can keep track of people living on properties, homeowners, and property managers. CodeNForce also helps store contact information for those assiociated with a property like mailing address, phone number(s), and email addresses.
