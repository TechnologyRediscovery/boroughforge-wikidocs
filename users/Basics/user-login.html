User Login

Made by Natalie Merola with Scribe



1. Navigate to https://www.google.com/ or your preferred search engine.

2. Search for "CodeNForce"

3. Click "codeNforcecodeNforcehttps://codenforce.org"

4. Click "codeNforce"

5. Click "Login To Mission Control"

6. Enter in your Username and Password

Tip: Tip! Save this information in your browser for easy access later on. This will also be your login for the app.

7. Select the municipality in which you are working in

Tip: Tip! COG Land is the test database. Here, you can experiment and practice different functions without risking any "real" data.

Tip: Tip! You can "Bookmark" the municipality landing page for easy access later on. For Google, simply select the star next to the web address and save to your "bookmark bar".
