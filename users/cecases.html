Code Enforcement Cases

codeNforce supports the following general case management flows:

  1. Open a case from a street patrol, a failed inspection, action request from the muni staff, or the public
  2. Conduct a field inspection
  3. Identify violated ordinances
  4. Log events
  5. Send a notice or a letter
  6. Create and track citations
  7. Store photos and documents

 

Caseload manager

The dashboard features a caseload manager which categorizes your municipality's open cases into their priority bins and offers a suite of quick actions directly from the individual case cards: closing, letter viewing, finalizing, and marking as sent, event logging.

Initiate batch case operations directly from the caseload manager. As of March 2026, only case closure is an available batch action.
